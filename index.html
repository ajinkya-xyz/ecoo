About Us

Rooted in Daund,
Serving Industry

ECOO Alternate Fuels India Pvt. Ltd. was incorporated on 30th December 2023 with a clear mission: to provide environmentally sound pre-processing of hazardous industrial waste and convert it into certified alternate fuels for cement kilns across India.

Operating from Plot A 93/5, MIDC Kurkumbh, Daund, Pune — our facility is strategically located in one of Maharashtra's premier industrial zones, enabling efficient waste collection and processing from industries across Pune, Nasik, Thane, Raigad and beyond.

We work in strict compliance with the Hazardous & Other Wastes (Management & Transboundary Movement) Rules, 2016 and operate under consent granted by the Maharashtra Pollution Control Board, valid through September 2029.

Our founding team brings together expertise from IT, chemical engineering, banking, and aviation — united by a shared commitment to transforming hazardous waste management in the region through innovation and responsibility.

Why Co-Processing in Cement Kilns?
♻️ Zero Residue
High-temperature kiln incineration at 1400°C+ ensures complete destruction of hazardous organics with zero residue — unlike dedicated incinerators that produce ash requiring further disposal.
🌍 Fossil Fuel Substitution
Every tonne of alternate fuel used displaces coal or petcoke, reducing CO₂ emissions, conserving natural resources, and helping cement plants achieve their sustainability goals.
📋 Full Regulatory Compliance
Co-processing is authorized under CPCB guidelines (July 2017) and HWM Rules 2016 — providing a safe, legal, and documented disposal pathway for industries.

Our Processing Workflow
01
Waste Collection & Transport
Authorized hazardous waste transporters collect from client industries. MOU-backed, MPCB compliant transportation chain.
02
Characterization & Segregation
Metal detection, EM separation; disc/rotary/trommel screening for waste stream identification and classification.
03
Pre-Processing & Dehydration
Blending, size reduction, dehydration and moisture control to optimize calorific value and handling characteristics.
04
Alternate Fuel Formulation
Precise blending to meet cement plant specifications — calorific value, chlorine content, moisture, and particle size.
05
Dispatch to Cement Co-Processors
Certified alternate fuel dispatched to authorized cement plants — Dalmia, JK Cement, Wonder Cement and ACC.
