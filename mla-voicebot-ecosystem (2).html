02 Use Cases — Inbound & Outbound
📥
Inbound
Citizen Calls MLA Office
  • Report roads / potholes / streetlights not working
  • Water supply & drainage complaints
  • Request government scheme assistance (PM Awas, Ration etc.)
  • Land record disputes and revenue issues

  • Health & hospital services complaints

  • Event / program invitations to constituents
  • Voter welfare scheme awareness calls
  • Pre-election pulse survey (issue tracking)
  • Feedback on newly completed projects (roads, schools)
  • Emergency alert / broadcast to specific areas
03 Multilingual Intelligence Layer

Auto Language Detection & Code-Switching

The bot identifies which language the caller is speaking within the first few words — no IVR menu needed ("Press 1 for Hindi"). It responds in the same language throughout the conversation. If a caller code-switches mid-conversation (common in Maharashtra — mixing Marathi + Hindi + English), the NLU handles it gracefully.


Example: A caller starts in Marathi, switches to Hindi to explain a technical term — the bot continues to respond in Marathi, understanding both.

Auto-Categorization & Routing
AI tags category, subcategory and priority. Routes to relevant department (PWD, Health, Revenue etc.).

Staff Dashboard Alert
MLA office staff see new ticket in dashboard. WhatsApp / email notification sent to assigned team.

Staff updates ticket status (In Progress → Resolved). Citizen can check status via SMS reply or missed call.

Ticket Closed + Report
Ticket closed with full audit trail. Monthly reports generated for MLA review.
05 Outbound Campaign Flows
🔊 AI Outbound Voice Campaigns
Three ready-to-deploy outbound call flows that can be launched from the dashboard
✅
Issue Resolution Verification
  • Bot calls citizen 48–72 hrs after ticket marked resolved
  • Asks: "Was your reported issue resolved?"
  • If NO → ticket automatically re-opened and escalated
  • If YES → collects 1–5 satisfaction rating
  • Asks if any other issue to report
  • Full response logged back to ticket
📊
Constituency Survey
  • Admin uploads survey questions via dashboard
  • Voter list imported (filtered by village / ward)
  • Bot calls in Marathi / Hindi based on area
  • Asks 3–5 questions, one at a time
  • Responses captured as structured data
  • Live results dashboard updated in real-time
📢
Awareness & Broadcast
  • MLA records message or AI generates voice
  • Personalised: "नमस्कार [Name]जी, आमदार बोलत आहे..."
  • Announce events, scheme deadlines, inaugurations
  • Can target specific villages or wards
  • Delivery report: connected / not connected / callback
  • Opt-out management built-in

09 Compliance, Privacy & Security
📜
TRAI Compliance
DLT-registered SMS sender ID, DPDP-compliant call recording consent, outbound call timings (9am–9pm)
🔐
Data Security
AES-256 encryption for call recordings. Caller phone numbers masked in tickets. RBAC for staff access.
🇮🇳
Data Residency
All data hosted on AWS/Azure India regions (Mumbai). No data sent outside India. Citizen PII protected.
🎙️
Call Consent
Bot announces recording at call start. Outbound calls include opt-out option. Consent stored per interaction.
